made in visual studios 2019.16.7.1


A text-based RPG shop simulator 

type out commands to purchase item for the shop

There are 3 menus

The main menu is where you can quit the game, check your inventory or purchase items which
will lead you to the next menu.

The weapons/potions menu is where you choose to either purchase from a list of weapons or a list of potions,
you can type quit to retuen to main menu.

the purchase menu is where where you can purchase items by typing whatever is on the list, you can 
type quit to return back to main menu.
